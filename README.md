# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes workforce data across 1,200 employee records to identify employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, departmental performance, and compensation insights. The dashboard was designed to support HR decision-making through interactive visualizations and KPI reporting.

## KPIs

* Total Employees: 1,200
* Attrition Count: 219
* Attrition Rate: 18.25%
* Average Monthly Income: $6,404.94

## Dashboard Features

* Employee Attrition by Department
* Employee Attrition by Job Role
* Employee Distribution by Age Group
* Monthly Income by Attrition Status
* Department Filter
* Business Insights Summary

## Key Insights

* Attrition Rate: 18.25%
* Human Resources experienced the highest attrition count.
* Employees aged 36–55 represent the largest workforce segments.
* Managers and Human Resources roles have the highest employee counts.
* Employees who left earned slightly lower average monthly income than retained employees.
